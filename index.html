About Me

My name is Jie Qi, a PhD candidate in Geotechnical Engineering at the University of Melbourne. I specialize in fluid flow in granular materials, DEM/CFD simulations, pore network modeling, and machine learning applications in geomechanics.

Research Works

  • Hydraulic Anisotropy in Granular Assemblies – An experimental and pore-network modeling study.
  • Internal Erosion in Gap-Graded Soils – CFD-DEM simulations and autoencoder-based anomaly detection.
  • TiGeoNet Framework – A Taichi-accelerated pipeline for pore-scale network feature extraction.
